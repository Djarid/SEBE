# GPEW Reference Documents

Internal Green Party of England and Wales governance documents.
These are party-owned materials, not for public redistribution.
All files in this directory (except this index) are gitignored.

## Documents

| File | Description | Pages | Version/Date |
|---|---|---|---|
| `Constitution after Autumn 2025 v2.pdf` | GPEW Constitution (full, post-Autumn Conference 2025). Covers party structure, GPC, GPEx, leader elections, conference procedures, policy-making (Section 14), campaigning, ADR, equalities. | 35 | Autumn 2025 |
| `SOCC-after-Autumn-2022.pdf` | Standing Orders for the Conduct of Conference. Conference agenda process, motion submission, amendments, voting procedures, elections at conference. Includes Appendix A (Policy Process). | 24 | Autumn 2022 |

| `Constitution Tech Digital Greens-1.pdf` | Constitution for the Tech and Digital Greens Special Interest Group (SIG). Purpose, membership, scope, structure. Working title: Tech & Digital Greens / Green Digital Network. | 4 | August 2025 |

## Relevance to SEBE

These documents define the procedural path for getting SEBE adopted as party policy:

- **Constitution Section 14** defines how policy is made (PWGs, voting papers, conference motions)
- **SOCC** specifies how to submit motions to conference, amendment rules, and voting procedures
- **PDC SOs** detail how the Policy Development Committee manages working groups and policy development
- **Digital Greens SIG** is a potential coalition partner (tech policy alignment, Lumin's Discord invite)
