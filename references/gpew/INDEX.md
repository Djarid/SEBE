## Relevance to SEBE

These documents define the procedural path for getting SEBE adopted as party policy:

- **Constitution Section 14** defines how policy is made (PWGs, voting papers, conference motions)
- **SOCC** specifies how to submit motions to conference, amendment rules, and voting procedures
- **PDC SOs** detail how the Policy Development Committee manages working groups and policy development
- **Digital Greens SIG** is a potential coalition partner (tech policy alignment, Lumin's Discord invite)
